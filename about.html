About PrimeEstate

Who We Are

RealEstate is a leading real estate company headquartered in Honnavar, Karnataka, India. Founded in 2026, we have grown from a small local agency into one of the most trusted names in property buying, selling, and renting across Karnataka.

Our team of 80+ experienced real estate professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional service, transparent transactions, and the best value for every client — whether you're a first-time buyer or a seasoned investor.

We specialise in residential villas, apartments, independent houses, and commercial spaces across premium locations.
